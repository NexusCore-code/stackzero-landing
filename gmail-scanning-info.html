What is Gmail Scanning?

Gmail scanning is an optional feature that lets StackZero automatically analyze your software subscriptions. It works by scanning your inbox for receipts, billing emails, and subscription confirmations. This helps us generate a much more accurate and personalized savings report for you.

How it works:

  • Read-only access: we never modify, send, or delete anything
  • Limited scope: only emails related to subscriptions are processed
  • No personal content: private conversations and attachments are ignored
  • Fully revocable: you can remove access at any time via your Google account

Gmail scanning is completely optional. You will be able to choose whether to enable it when you upgrade your report. No action will be taken unless you explicitly check that option.

If you ever change your mind, you can revoke access here.

StackZero uses secure OAuth login from Google and never requests your password. All processing is automatic, secure, and private.
