What is StackZero?

StackZero is an AI-powered service that helps businesses and individuals analyze and optimize their SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) expenses. Whether you're managing subscriptions across tools like Notion, Slack, Figma, Zoom, or others — we help you identify:

  • Unused or forgotten subscriptions
  • Duplicate services across teams
  • Overpriced tools you could replace or downscale

You get a clean, personalized PDF report — perfect to share with finance, operations, or your team.
